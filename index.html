Plot


The Group Fall Out Boy starts off getting kidnapped by a mysterious group of females and become tortured captives. Throughout their adventure, they encounter multiple allies who help them escape and enemies who side with the they battle a music hating cult. Through the blood and pain, the band attempts to save rock and roll forever.
